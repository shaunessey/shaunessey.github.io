Double Bass

I play the Double Bass in the Butte Symphony Orchestra.
Most Double Basses are tune E-A-D-G, but I tune mine C-G-D-A, like a Cello, but an octave lower. If you want to hear the bass played in fifths by a world-class musician, then you should listen to Joel Quarrington!
