Shadowrun

Shadowrun is a tabletop role-playing game that combines the genres of cyberpunk and fantasy. It is set in a near-future world (the "Sixth World") where magic has returned and blended with advanced technology. Players take on the roles of shadowrunners, mercenary specialists who undertake dangerous, illegal missions. My group's setting - and most of Shadowrun, is set in the greater Seattle area in the late 21st century.

Shadowrunners often operate in the shadows, taking on jobs like corporate espionage, extraction, or even assassinations. They use a variety of skills, from high-tech hacking (the second iteration of the internet is called "the Matrix") and combat prowess, to sepll-casting and magical assistance.

Key elements of the setting include megacorporations that are above the law, the return of classic fantasy races (such as Elves, Dwarves, Orks, Trolls, Goblins, and Vampires), and sophisticated cybernetic and bioware enhancements - along with the ability to cast spells and summon spirits. This combination of tech and magic is what makes the world so unique and exciting for roleplaying, but makes for a complex and daunting rule system.

       
                   
       

Choose, Raven Shaman

       

Pronounced CHO-seg. Raven Shaman. Choose is a retired detective from Wyoming. He has studied the shamanic arts of the Raven and offers them gifts in exchange for magical favors, such as Spirit Sight.

       

Choose is a kind-hearted man and a clever detective. He uses the crows and ravens of the city like an underground network of winged spies.

       
       
               
       

Gypsy, Combat Medic

       

Previously employed by Trauma Team, the privately-owned medical response contractor (which is like a private ambulance company that responds to medical emergencies like a SWAT team), Gypsy is a wise-cracking know-it-all, based off of Kurt Russell's character in "Big Trouble in Little China".

       

Enhanced with bionics, Gypsy is unnaturally fast, strong, and accurate.

Gypsy's best friend is a Sasquatch named Hindenburg, who lives in the public parks of Seattle where he tends to plants.

   
       
       
                   
       

Inmutanka, Physical Adept

       

Pronounced in-moo-TAHN-ka. Shark Spirit Physical Adept. Inmutanka was a member of the Sioux Nations' Special Forces unit, before defecting and fleeing to Seattle. Part of a corporate conspiracy, his team was murdered by bionic corporate hitmen after a successful secret mission.

       

Inmutanka has an upbeat, positive personality but when in combat, his Shark Spirit influence takes over and he quickly becomes bloodthirsty.
