The Cure

... to the Common Death Curse

This band of adventurers got their start when Jasker accepted a contract from a dying woman: find the source of the Death Curse and remove it!

Since then, "The Cure" have driven through the jungles of Chult on Hadrosaurus back searching for a hidden dungeon where they will confront the evil Lich Acererack.
